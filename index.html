Who I am

Hello, I’m Julia!

I’m a UX Designer with a background in print and media technologies. During my study at Hochschule München, I got practical skills not only in logo creation and layout design but also in book manufacturing. I know that every book starts with an idea, but its success involves discipline, management, and creativity and this approach helps me to benefit also as a UX Designer.
Read More

During my work on my bachelor thesis, I was designing a User Interface for a jewelry online shop. I was looking for solutions that could help me to make my online shop better and have found psychological studies published on the Nielsen Norman Group page. I realized it’s not enough to create only a visually beautiful design, it should be above all functional and user-friendly. I’ve dipped myself into UX design and now I know why I’ve changed to UX - I like the ability to focus on the people and that is why my solutions consider ease of use and accessibility. I want to be a part of a team in which we can develop intuitive and trendy products customers can rely on.

As a UX/UI designer, I can bring a problem-solving and user-centered approach to work and the ability to create order from chaos.
