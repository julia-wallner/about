Designer's code of ethics

As designer I undertake to act with professional responsibility in the interests of ecology and environmental protection.

As designer I would not, directly or indirectly, try to exclude another designer from work, and also for the purpose of competition, reduce the size of the fee or resort to other dishonest methods.

As designer I have to be fair in criticism; I have no right to tarnish either the work or the reputation of his fellow designer.

As designer I have no right to follow the instructions of the client if this leads to the creation of plagiarism, as well as deliberately create plagiarism.

As designer I would not perform work that deliberately harms or threatens the privacy or safety of members of the public or of a community involved in the project.

As designer I would not participate in or condone discrimination based on race, sex, age, religion, national origin, sexual orientation, disability, or financial status.

As designer I will do my best work on every project and give every project the full attention it deserves.
